About the movie

"Raazi" is an espionage thriller that follows Sehmat, a young Indian woman who marries a Pakistani military officer to serve as an undercover spy during the 1971 India-Pakistan war. Driven by patriotism, she faces numerous challenges while gathering information for India. The film showcases her courage and the emotional turmoil of her dual life. It is based on the novel "Calling Sehmat" by Harinder S. Sikka.
