About the movie

"Stree 2" continues the story of the mysterious spirit "Stree" who terrorizes a small town by abducting men at night. Set in the same quirky and eerie universe, the sequel delves deeper into the origins of Stree and her motives. The townspeople, along with Vicky and his friends, must find a way to end the haunting for good. It combines horror, humor, and drama with an engaging narrative.
