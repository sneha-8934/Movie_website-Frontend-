About the movie

"Chennai Express" is a comedy-adventure film that follows Rahul, who sets out to fulfill his late grandfather's wish of immersing his ashes. During his journey on the Chennai Express, he inadvertently becomes involved in a series of comical and dangerous situations involving a runaway woman, Meenamma, from a powerful South Indian family. Filled with action, humor, and romance, the movie showcases a delightful culture clash.
